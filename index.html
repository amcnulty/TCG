About Us

The Contractor's Garage offers large usable industrial storage with spaces in Johnson County, Kansas and Northland Kansas City, designed exclusively for entrepreneurs and hobbyists including but not limited to landscapers, plumbers, electricians, specialty vehicles, or anyone requiring space larger and more flexible than mini storage yet smaller and less expensive than traditional incubator industrial space.

We currently have five locations with a 6th location under construction south of Liberty's 152 highway exit visible west of I-35 and Flintlock. All units are insulated with electricity and have large drive-in doors and loading lots for trailer maneuverability. Garage 5 also features a storefront with extra front-entry parking, a restroom, spiral ducted HVAC, and polished concrete floors more traditional of office/industrial incubator space. Unlike mini storage, our units are built with the contractor in mind!
